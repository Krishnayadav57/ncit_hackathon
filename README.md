# ASURA

ASURA is an AI-powered mental wellness RPG designed for students. Instead of treating stress, anxiety, procrastination, loneliness, and exam pressure as invisible problems, ASURA transforms them into a living "Asura" that players can understand and overcome.

Players strengthen their Resolve and weaken their Asura through real-life healthy habits such as breathing exercises, focus sessions, quality sleep, journaling, and self-reflection. These actions become in-game attacks, turning wellness into an engaging and rewarding experience.

ASURA features adaptive enemies, cooperative guilds, weekly leagues, exam raid bosses, and AI-driven voice journaling to encourage connection, balance, and long-term mental well-being. Rather than destroying the Asura, players eventually learn to live with and manage it, transforming it into a companion that helps them recognize unhealthy patterns.

Built with AI and game mechanics, ASURA aims to make mental health support accessible, engaging, and culturally meaningful for students while ensuring that serious crises are met with real human support.
